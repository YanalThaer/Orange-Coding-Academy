LINER EQUATION

An equation that has maximum degree one is known as liner equation. this graph of it is a straight line.

For example

  • y = 8x - 2
  • ax1 = bx2 - cx3 = 9

    • are some of the examples of a liner equation in two and three variables

    Slop of intersection of two liner equation
    y1 = mx1 + a and
    y2 = mx2 + b
    for the points P(x1 , y1) and Q(2 , y2) is:
    m = (y2 - y1)/(x2 - x1)
